Space Management

About Us

The office supports those who design, build or maintain buildings as well as many others in the VCU community by....

  • Archiving, verifying and maintaining all buidling related information including room inventory data, architectural drawings and spec books as accurately as possible and sharing this infomation in an easy-to-access and user friendly manner.
  • Maintaining the VCU room inventory database and related space reporting web sites.
  • Providing GIS/mapping services to the VCU community including the Board of Visitors.
