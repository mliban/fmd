Physical Plant maintains all university grounds and landscaping. Services include:

  • Leaf/litter collection
  • Landscape design, installation and maintenance
  • Turfgrass management
  • Landscape pest management
  • Tree installation and maintenance
  • Irrigation management
  • Concrete, asphalt and paver installation and maintenance
  • Snow removal and ice control
  • Plan review

Grounds Department staff includes a Virginia Master Gardener and two ISA Certified Arborists.
