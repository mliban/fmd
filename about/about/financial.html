Financial Services

Financial Services provides operational and reporting services. These services include:

  • Divisionwide human resource and payroll transaction processing
  • Asset costing of labor and materials
  • Utility invoice payment
  • Accounts payable oversight
  • Customer billing for services rendered by the Facilities Management Division

Financial Services provides internal and external financial reporting of division operations.

Billing cycle

Facilities Management bills on a weekly basis (usually every Friday). If you have any questions, please feel free to contact our office.
