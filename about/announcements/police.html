Police Firing Range - Environmental Assessment - Public Comment Period

« Back to Announcements/Reports

Virginia Commonwealth University proposes to install a pistol firing range in the basement of the new VCU Police Department Headquarters on East Broad Street. Installation of the firing range has been made possible by a grant from the United States Department of the Treasury. Toward complying with Federal regulations for awarding such grants, VCU prepared an Environmental Assessment of the proposed project, which was submitted to Treasury for review and approval. Federal regulations also require that the Environmental Assessment report be provided to the public, for comment, prior to issuing final approval of the project.

The range will provide necessary training for VCU Police on an ongoing basis and will improve the safety and effectiveness of the sworn officers. The grant was awarded to fund the capital costs of the construction of the range at the new VCU Police Headquarters.

The Environmental Assessment revealed no adverse environmental effects in connection with the proposed project. The full report may be accessed via the following link: Click Here

A hard copy is also available for review at the University's Planning and Design Office, 700 West Grace Street, Suite 1500, Richmond, Virginia 23284. A 30-day public comment period is open and ends June 3rd, 2015 wherein the public is invited to submit comments on the proposed project and EA document.
