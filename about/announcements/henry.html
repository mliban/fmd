Welcome Dana Henry, Human Resource Manager for Facilities Management

« Back to Announcements/Reports

It is with great enthusiasm that we welcome Dana Henry, who recently joined VCU as the Human Resource Manager for Facilities Management. She has 15 years of human resources experience including recruitment, employee relations, performance management, benefits administration, compensation, policy development, training and development, and legal compliance. Dana graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University with a M.Ed. in Adult Education and Human Resource Development, a Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Human Resource Development, and a B.S. in Mass Communications. She is certified as a Professional in Human Resources (PHR), a SHRM Senior Certified Professional (SHRM-SCP), and an IPMA-HR Senior Certified Professional (IPMA-SCP).
